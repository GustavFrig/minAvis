Samsung's new phone with built in AI-abilities

The new AI features

The Samsung Galaxy S24 series introduces several impressive AI-driven features. One of the standout additions is "Circle to Search," which allows users to simply draw a circle around any content they want more information about. Without needing to switch apps, AI instantly provides relevant search results, making it a seamless and intuitive tool. Another major AI enhancement is "Generative Edit," a feature that enables users to remove unwanted elements from photos directly within the camera app. This creates a Photoshop-like experience without requiring advanced editing skills.

Other-than-AI improvements

Beyond AI, the Galaxy S24 also delivers significant improvements in performance and user experience. Powered by a custom-built Snapdragon 8 processor, the device ensures smooth and responsive usage. The display and graphics have been enhanced, offering a brighter screen and sharper visuals for an overall improved viewing experience.
