Deep Seek: Revolution within AI-Tech

What is Deep Seek?

DeepSeek is an advanced AI model developed in China, known for its energy efficiency and open-source approach. Launched in January 2025, it quickly gained popularity for competing with established models like ChatGPT at a lower cost. DeepSeek offers several specialized AI models, including DeepSeek-R1 for reasoning and DeepSeek-V3 for general use.

How does it work?

DeepSeek uses a self-reinforced learning model and a "Mixture-of-Experts" approach. This enables it to deliver precise and insightful answers without requiring much computing power. It is designed to handle complex tasks such as mathematical reasoning, coding, and content generation. The model is also available via a mobile app, web interface, and API.

What does this mean for the future?

DeepSeek has the potential to reshape the AI landscape by making advanced technology more accessible and cost-effective. It challenges established AI giants and could drive increased competition and innovation in the industry. With its energy-efficient approach, it may also contribute to more sustainable development in artificial intelligence.
