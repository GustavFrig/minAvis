Switch 2: Nintendo's new console

What's improved on the new switch?

The Switch 2 is supposed to be a pure upgrade from the original console that was released on the 3. of march, 2017. The Switch 2 is said to be larger than the original, up to 8 inches from the original 7, and there are rumors that it will be more powerful than the original, which may allow for gaming on 60fps.

The new console also has a new fastening mechanism, with the controllers connecting to the console by magnets. The buttons on the controllers have gotten bigger, which makes playing on just one controller much easier.

Price and release date

Nintendo has announced that the Switch 2 will release sometime this year, but a specific date has not been mentioned. However, more information will be released by Nintendo the 2. of april. The price is estimated to be between 399$-499$.

Summary

    - Switch 2 is a more powerful, bigger and faster version of the original.

    - The console will be released this year, but more information about the release date will be announced 2. of april.

    - The price point is around 399$-499$
